Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique-Ingres

The potrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and
today hangs in the Louvre.It is the third of three potraits of the rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date:1806
Medium:Oil on Canvas
Dimensions:100 x 70 cm
Home:Musèe du Louvre, Paris

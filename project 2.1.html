    Description

    The Mysore Palace is a historical palace and the royal residence at Mysore in the Indian State of Karnataka.

    It is the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore. The palace is in the centre of Mysore, and faces the Chamundi Hills eastward. Mysore is commonly described as the 'City of Palaces'.
